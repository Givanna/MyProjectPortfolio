Greyhound Acclimation

Scope

The project aims to help these dogs acclimate to new environmental stimuli, maintain their health, and prepare them for foster care or adoption.

Deliverables

  • Detailed schedule for evening walks and outdoor play sessions.
  • Comprehensive health reports for each Greyhound.
  • Daily records of kennel cleaning and maintenance.
  • Detailed reports on each Greyhound's adjustment to new stimuli.
  • Logs of communication with veterinary offices and foster/adoption coordinators.

Challenges and Solutions

  • Managing the stress and anxiety of Greyhounds adjusting to a new environment.
  • Identifying and addressing any behavioral issues that arise.
  • Ensuring timely detection and treatment of any medical issues.
  • Coordinating effectively with veterinary offices for regular check-ups and emergencies.
  • Ensuring safe and controlled exposure to new environments and situations.

Results

  • Successful Acclimatization:Greyhounds show reduced stress and anxiety, adapting well to their new environment.
  • Improved Health Outcomes:Timely medical interventions and consistent health monitoring lead to healthier Greyhounds.
  • Increased Adoption Rates:Well-adjusted and healthy Greyhounds are more likely to be adopted or fostered.
  • Achieved stakeholder approval and support, resulting in a well-received and maintained Knowledge Base.
  • Enhanced Reporting and Communication:Clear and comprehensive reports facilitate better decision-making and coordination among stakeholders.
