Executive Laptop Replacement

Scope

To promptly deliver, install, and configure a high-quality laptop for an executive experiencing hardware failure. This project aims to navigate current equipment delays and shortages while aligning with the executive's operational requirements and adhering to budget constraints.

Deliverables

  • Develop and share a comprehensive proposal that includes the necessity of expedited shipping due to equipment delays.
  • Communicate the need for expedited shipping with stakeholders and obtain necessary approvals for the additional costs.
  • Purchase the replacement laptop(s) aligning with the executiveâ€™s operational requirements.
  • Test the laptop thoroughly to ensure all necessary software and tools are functioning correctly.
  • Ensure prompt delivery of the laptop(s) despite current equipment delays.
  • Ensure the new laptop is configured correctly to meet the executive's requirements.
  • Maintain records of approvals, procurement, and equipment tracking.

Challenges

  • Expedited shipping was not included in the original budget approval.
  • Need to communicate this requirement to stakeholders and receive approval for the additional cost.
  • Navigating through current market delays and shortages to ensure timely procurement and delivery of the laptop.

Results

  • More laptops were purchased to ensure readiness for any future executive hardware failures.
  • Relevant contracts were renewed to support the new hardware.
  • The executive experienced minimal disruption to daily responsibilities due to the efficient configuration and installation of the new laptop.
