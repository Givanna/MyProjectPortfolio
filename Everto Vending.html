Everto Vending

Scope

The EVERTO VENDING project aims to create a sustainable and profitable vending business. The scope includes the acquisition of vending machines, securing prime locations, building a client base, and ensuring compliance with all relevant regulations. The project will involve scouting for locations, online marketing efforts, acquiring necessary equipment and materials, and applying for grants to support the business financially.

Deliverables

  • Identify and secure high-traffic locations for vending machines.
  • Develop and implement an online marketing strategy to attract clients and promote the business.
  • Purchase and install vending machines and stock them with products.
  • Research and apply for relevant grants to support business operations and expansion.
  • Establish and maintain a risk management plan for the duration of the project.

Challenges

  • Managing financial resources effectively to cover all necessary expenses.
  • Navigating the application process and meeting eligibility requirements for grants.
  • Ensuring consistent availability of products to meet customer demand and avoid stockouts.

Risk Mitigation

  • Obtain comprehensive business insurance that covers general liability, property damage, and business interruption.
  • Diversified Income Streams
  • Financial planning includes an emergency fund.
  • Equipment maintenance.
  • Stay informed about state and local regulations, ensuring the business remains compliant and retain legal counsel.

    • Results

    • Successfully acquired three prime locations within one week of scouting.
    • Achieved 40% more than the expected monthly income.
    • Completed all deliverables except for obtaining grants.

    Lessons Learned

    • Early and thorough scouting can lead to securing high-traffic locations quickly, which significantly boosts revenue potential.
    • A robust online marketing strategy is crucial for attracting clients and building brand awareness.
    • Managing a limited budget requires meticulous planning and prioritization of essential expenses.
